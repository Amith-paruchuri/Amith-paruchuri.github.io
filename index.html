Brainâ€“Retina Biological Aging Coupling (Submitted to AAIC 2026)

This project investigates whether biological aging is reflected systemically across different organs by studying the coupling between retinal and brain aging signatures. Independent aging clocks were developed from retinal fundus imaging using pretrained foundation models and from structural brain imaging features. The core objective was to determine whether deviations from chronological age in these two modalities converge or diverge across the lifespan.

The analysis involved residualized modeling, interaction analysis, and spline-based nonlinear regression to examine cross-modal relationships while accounting for age effects. This work demonstrates how multimodal imaging can capture shared aging biology.

Lymphocyte Infiltration at Tumor Border in DLBCL (USCAP 2026)

This computational pathology project quantifies how lymphocytes infiltrate tumor borders in DLBCL whole-slide images. Tumor and cellular compartments were segmented using deep learning, and a transition zone band was created around tumor margins.

Spatial infiltration patterns were modeled as continuous curves and slope-based features were extracted to objectively represent tumorâ€“immune topology. These features capture biologically meaningful spatial heterogeneity not visible through bulk immune density.

Tools: Python, TIAToolbox, CellViT, OpenCV

AML Transcriptomic Genomic Risk Score (ASCO 2026)

This work develops a transcriptome-derived genomic risk score capable of capturing immune regulatory and leukocyte differentiation programs beyond traditional cytogenetic risk stratification. Survival modeling and LASSO regression were used to derive gene signatures.

Differential expression and pathway enrichment analyses were used to characterize immune signaling landscapes underlying adverse prognosis.

Tools: R, limma, glmnet, survival, clusterProfiler

Immune Activation Index in Coeliac Disease (DDW 2026)

This study investigates mucosal transcriptomic landscapes in coeliac disease using differential gene expression and immune deconvolution. An immune activation index was derived to quantify mucosal immune state beyond histology.

Pathway enrichment highlighted inflammatory signaling, antigen presentation, and lymphoid infiltration correlating with disease activity.

Tools: R, limma, xCell, clusterProfiler

Dementia Stage Classification from MRI using MobileNet (AAN 2026)

Transfer learning using MobileNet was applied to structural MRI to classify stages of dementia. Grad-CAM visualization was used to confirm spatial consistency of attention with disease- relevant anatomical regions.

EEG Spectrogram Classification for Schizophrenia (AAAI 2026)

EEG signals were converted into spectrogram representations and classified using deep learning architectures to develop an objective screening tool for schizophrenia, particularly suited for low-resource settings.

Mitotic Cell Detection using SAM + ResNet

Mitotic figures are critical prognostic markers in several tumors. In this project, RGB crops around cell coordinates were generated and fused with SAM-derived segmentation masks to train a ResNet classifier for mitotic detection.
